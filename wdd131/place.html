About Sierra Leone

Sierra Leone is a beautiful West African country known for its stunning beaches, lush landscapes, and vibrant culture. It gained independence from Britain in 1961 and has since been working toward economic growth and national unity. The capital city, Freetown, is famous for its historical connection to the transatlantic slave trade and is home to Fourah Bay College, the oldest university in West Africa. With a population rich in diversity and resilience, Sierra Leone continues to develop as a peaceful and welcoming nation.
